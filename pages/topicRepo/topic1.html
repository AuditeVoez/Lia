Topic 1

Describe a situation where data is collected and transformed into knowledge. Explain each transformation stage.

Say I'm a student working on my Mathematics in the Modern World project. In my laptop, I search up a statistics set and begin my search for datasets that I need to analyze. Going onto reputable sites, I would be presented by raw data. To me, these would be numbers that are purely statistical and have no direct meaning to me yet.

I would then start to interpret that data, using mathematical formulas and logical connections to turn that data into information. Information is basically raw data processed and turned into something more useable. It's kind of like making food, where data feels like raw meat, and by cooking (processing) it, you get something edible (information). After I process the data, there's one more step I need to take. Just like cooking meat, it won't be complete unless I personalize it, and in this case, I will be turning the information into knowledge.

Knowledge, as defined, is information you personalize and process individually.
